about Me

Hello! My name is Ali Hassan, a passionate Full Stack Web and Mobile Hybrid App Developer with over 5 years of rich experience. I have a history of transforming intricate ideas into dynamic web, mobile, and desktop applications.
Passionate about creating robust, scalable, and maintainable software solutions that meet business needs. With 5+ years of experience, I possess expertise in JavaScript and TypeScript, coupled with in-depth knowledge of popular front-end frameworks such as React and Angular. My goal is to develop intuitive and responsive user interfaces that provide a seamless experience to users.

Proficient in Redux Toolkit, I efficiently manage state in complex applications, ensuring smooth and glitch-free experiences. On the back-end, I excel in Node.js and Express.js, building APIs that communicate seamlessly with databases like MongoDB, Postgres, and Redis for efficient data storage and retrieval.

With hands-on experience in AWS services including EC2, S3, and Lambda, I have the ability to build scalable and reliable web applications that effortlessly handle heavy traffic loads. My skill set extends to real-time, event-driven applications utilizing Socket.IO, providing users with instant updates and notifications.

I am proficient at ensuring the quality and reliability of applications through rigorous testing methodologies. My proficiency in Jest and Enzyme allows for precise unit and integration testing, while Cypress provides robust end-to-end testing frameworks to simulate real-world user interactions and ensure application integrity under various scenarios. As a full stack JavaScript developer, I am well-versed in the entire development process, including version control, testing, and deployment. With strong problem-solving skills and a keen eye for optimization, I consistently identify and resolve complex issues efficiently.
